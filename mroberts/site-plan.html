About

My career started out in technology. Working with databases, web development, and then digital marketing. As the world has moved into the into age of big data and information management, data analysis has become invaluable in making informed business decisions. I love a good mystery and feel energized when it comes to exploring, analyzing, and finding answers. The entire data analysis process is exciting from asking questions, finding data, data wrangling and cleaning to the actual analysis and visualization. My skillset is in mergeing the world of technology and business by asking the right questions, finding the the right data, and creating meaniningful insights and visualizations.
