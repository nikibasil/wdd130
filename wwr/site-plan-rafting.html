Purpose

The purpose of this website is to bring more business into our compnay by providing information on our services and tours. We need to website so new customers can find us when they are searching for information on white water rafting tours, and are able to see what we have to offer. This will benefit the company by creating new customers and generating more income.

Audience

Our audience are people that are looking for adventure. People that enjy active adventure and exciting vacations an experiences. Probably a younger crowd, and groups and organizations. Companieses may also want to use us for team building and as a benefit for their employees. There including different services and levels of pricing. It will also have a way to contact the compnay for more information. It will be easy to use and user friendly. Out website will be modern with modern fonts, styles, and a lot of images that would draw this kind of crowd to our site. It will also be professional. It will have color paletteand site map that viewers can access. It will also be responsive as many of my users will be on smaller dsevices as phones, tablets, and laptops.
